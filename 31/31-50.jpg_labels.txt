272,494,AH 14\, Royal Wizard Guinille\, under the help of Master Wizard Rick James\, created the Old Imperial City Wizard Academy. For 10 years\, it was supported by the court and the Church. In AH24\, the Academy was renamed to Old Imperial Library. Rick James passed away in AH28. His statue remains at the door of the Library. The day he was buried\, the Deity of Protection appeared in the sky. The church named him an Honorable Servant of God\, and buried him in the holy sites.\n
